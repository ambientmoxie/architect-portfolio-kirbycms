Hotnews:

From September 7 to 11, we'll be presenting a selection of our work at Paris Design Week, at the Espace Commines in the Marais district, on two separate stands:

Stand 1: Common Qualities - four designs
Gathered under the theme COMMON QUALITIES - FOUR DESIGNS, four chairs designed by four Parisian design, architecture or CGI artist studios take a sober, expressive look at the technical assembly of a design archetype. A back, a seat, legs: four variations. With Camille Viallet and Théo Leclercq (Our Society), Raphaël Boursier (Mesure Studio) and Simon Geringer.

Stand 2: Heger et Demumieux - Light Motiv
A selection of objects will be presented, including the resin version of the Toro chair and the Ariane luminaire. This selection follows on from the exhibitions at Milan Design Week and the Venice Design Biennale.
